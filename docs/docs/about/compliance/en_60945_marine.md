# EN 60945 (Marine Equipment)

## Scope
Marine navigation and radio communication equipment and systems — General requirements.

## Why it applies
Used by marine industry to qualify equipment for shipboard environments.

## Achieving compliance
- identify the applicable scope and limits for the product category;  
- test against the cited standard or arrange testing at an accredited laboratory as required; and  
- maintain records of results and design changes that affect compliance.

## Evidence to keep
- test plans, setups, and results;  
- supplier declarations and component data; and  
- version‑controlled design files and bills of materials.

## Marking and labelling
No specific mark beyond CE/UKCA where applicable; documentation should cite test results.
