# Power Sensor

The MDD400 continuously monitors its own supply voltage and current draw (using an I²C power sensor) to provide diagnostic data, support fault detection, and enable intelligent power management. See the [quick reference](../../quick_reference.md) for the ESP32-S3 GPIO allocations and a listing of all I²C devices and their addresses.
