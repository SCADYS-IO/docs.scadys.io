# CE Marking

The MDD400 will carry the CE mark to indicate compliance with applicable EU directives:

- Electromagnetic Compatibility Directive (2014/30/EU);
- Low Voltage Directive (2014/35/EU); and
- RoHS Directive (2011/65/EU).

Pre-compliance testing for emissions and immunity will be performed during pre-production. The device operates below 50 V DC and is classified as low voltage. Final conformity assessment and Declaration of Conformity will be issued prior to EU sales.
