# IP Rating

The MDD400 enclosure is designed to meet IP65 for protection against dust and water jets.

- the front surface includes a sealed LCD window and gasketed perimeter;
- all connectors are rated IP67 when mated;
- the buzzer includes a membrane vent; and
- internal pressure equalization is provided via a hydrophobic vent.

Ingress protection will be verified by third-party testing during pre-certification.
