# Compliance Overview

This section outlines the regulatory and environmental compliance requirements for the MDD400 marine digital display. Each subsection provides a summary of the applicable standard or directive and indicates current design alignment. Full certification will be undertaken closer to the product release.

Covered areas include:

- CE marking under the EU EMC and LVD directives;
- FCC Part 15 Class B for emissions in the United States;
- material and disposal regulations including RoHS, WEEE and REACH;
- enclosure ingress protection (IP) for marine use; and
- certification to the NMEA 2000 protocol and electrical specification.
