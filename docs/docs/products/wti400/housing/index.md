# Housing Overview

This section provides the enclosure-level context for WTI400, including connector access, mounting intent, and environmental considerations for marine installations.

## Design Objectives

* support practical retrofit installation in mixed-generation instrument systems;
* preserve clear access to NMEA 2000, legacy serial, and wind transducer interfaces;
* protect electronics from vibration, moisture ingress, and service handling.

## Integration Notes

Connector positions and domain boundaries are defined to reduce wiring ambiguity during commissioning and maintenance. Detailed connector definitions are available in the [WTI400 quick reference](../quick_reference.md).

## Status

Housing documentation is in progress and will be expanded with mechanical drawings, sealing details, and installation guidance as design revisions are finalized.
