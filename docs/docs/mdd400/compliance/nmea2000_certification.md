# NMEA 2000 Certification

The MDD400 is intended for certification under the NMEA 2000 standard.

- electrical interface conforms to ISO 11898-2 with galvanic isolation;
- network power draw remains within allowed limits for both LEN=1 and LEN=2;
- DeviceNet-compliant M12 connector is used;
- PGNs are assigned in accordance with MFD and general display categories.

Final NMEA certification will be coordinated with the National Marine Electronics Association prior to product release.
