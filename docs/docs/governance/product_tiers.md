# Product Tiers

SCADYS.io products are made available in different forms depending on the stage of development and the intended use. The following guide explains each tier so that customers can select the option that best fits their needs.  

## Pre-production prototypes
Pre-production prototypes are supplied free of charge to selected testers, makers, and reviewers. These units are intended for evaluation only.  

- status: engineering samples, not certified for retail use;  
- purpose: gather feedback on function and usability;  
- limitations: may not represent the final design, no warranty or support, and may not receive firmware or software updates after the prototyping stage; and  
- risk: changes may occur before retail release, and units may have issues that affect reliability.  

!!! warning
    *Pre-production prototypes are not supported after the prototyping stage.* 
    
    Firmware, software, and documentation updates may stop once retail versions are released.  
    Customers should not rely on these units for long-term use.  

## PCB-only versions
PCB-only versions are aimed at makers who want to assemble their own devices. The package includes a production-grade PCB with all surface-mount components assembled by a contract manufacturer. Through-hole components and external parts such as displays are supplied separately. STL files of the enclosure may also be provided for 3D printing.  

- status: not certified for compliance or retail sale;  
- purpose: allow hands-on assembly and experimentation;  
- limitations: requires soldering and assembly skills, customer provides housing and finishing; and  
- risk: incorrect assembly may damage parts or affect function.  

!!! info
    *PCB-only versions are supplied without compliance certification or warranty.*

    Assembly errors or design modifications may cause malfunction, and SCADYS.io cannot provide ongoing support for these units.  

## Full build kit
Full build kits contain everything required to assemble the device, including the PCB, all electronic components, housing, and hardware. Customers complete the final assembly themselves.  

- status: not certified for compliance or retail sale;  
- purpose: provide a near-complete product for hobbyists and advanced users;  
- limitations: requires mechanical assembly and attention to build instructions; and  
- risk: finished performance depends on customer workmanship, warranty is limited.  

!!! info
    *Full build kits are not certified for compliance.*

    SCADYS.io cannot guarantee performance or provide full warranty coverage for self-assembled kits.  
    Customers are responsible for correct assembly and safe operation.  

## Retail version
The retail version is the fully assembled and tested product supplied through regular sales channels. These units are certified for compliance in the regions where they are sold.  

- status: certified and approved for retail sale;  
- purpose: ready-to-use product for general customers;  
- limitations: availability begins after compliance testing and certification are complete; and  
- risk: none beyond normal consumer electronics use.  

!!! tip
    Retail versions are the only SCADYS.io products that are **fully certified, tested, and supported**.  
    Customers receive warranty coverage, eligibility for firmware and software updates, and assurance that the device complies with applicable standards in the regions where it is sold.
