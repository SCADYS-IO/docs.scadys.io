# Factory Calibration – ISO/IEC 17025

## Scope
ISO/IEC 17025 defines competence for testing and calibration laboratories.

## Why it applies
This is a market expectation rather than a legal requirement for placing products on the market.

## Achieving compliance
- identify the applicable scope and limits for the product category;  
- test against the cited standard or arrange testing at an accredited laboratory as required; and  
- maintain records of results and design changes that affect compliance.

## Evidence to keep
- test plans, setups, and results;  
- supplier declarations and component data; and  
- version‑controlled design files and bills of materials.

## Marking and labelling
No marking; keep calibration certificates and traceability statements.

## References
1. ISO/IEC, [*ISO/IEC 17025 — General requirements for the competence of testing and calibration laboratories*](https://www.iso.org/standard/66912.html), ISO, 2017
