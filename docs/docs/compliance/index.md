# Compliance

This section outlines the regulatory and environmental compliance objectives of the MDD400. It includes documentation and design considerations for meeting CE, FCC, and other international standards relevant to electromagnetic compatibility, safety, material restrictions, and environmental impact.

* *[CE Marking](ce_marking.md)*: Describes the design practices and test requirements related to European conformity under the EMC, LVD, and RED directives.

* *[FCC Compliance](fcc_compliance.md)*: Details the alignment with FCC Part 15B requirements for unintentional radiators, including conducted and radiated emissions testing.

* *[RoHS Compliance](rohs_compliance.md)*: Documents the product’s adherence to material restrictions on hazardous substances in electrical and electronic equipment.

* *[WEEE Compliance](weee_compliance.md)*: Covers product lifecycle and end-of-life disposal considerations in accordance with the Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment Directive.

* *[REACH Compliance](reach_compliance.md)*: Confirms product design avoids substances of very high concern (SVHCs) as defined under the EU REACH regulation.

* *[IP Rating](ip_rating.md)*: Defines the expected ingress protection level of the final enclosure and test approach for verifying resistance to dust and water.

* *[NMEA 2000 Certification](nmea2000_certification.md)*: Documents conformance to the NMEA 2000 physical and data layer specifications, including DeviceNet connector requirements and Load Equivalency Number (LEN) limits.

* *[Appendices](appendices.md)* and *[Change Log](changelog.md)*: Supplementary documentation and revision history for regulatory planning and test records.

This section supports internal validation, third-party testing, and market access certification.
