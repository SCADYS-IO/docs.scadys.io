## Pin Assignments

| GPIO | Net Label | Function |
|------|-----------|----------|
| GPIO 0 | ESP_BOOT | Boot mode select (hold low to enter download mode) |
| GPIO 1 | ST_EN | Legacy Serial TX driver enable |
| GPIO 8 | I2C_SCL | I²C clock |
| GPIO 9 | LED_EN | Status LED enable (active low) |
| GPIO 10 | AUDIO_PWM | Buzzer PWM output |
| GPIO 12 | TWAI_RX | CAN receive (TWAI peripheral) |
| GPIO 13 | TWAI_TX | CAN transmit (TWAI peripheral) |
| GPIO 14 | TWAI_EN | CAN transceiver standby (active low enable) |
| GPIO 18 | I2C_SDA | I²C data |
| GPIO 21 | DISP_EN | Display power enable (active high) |
| GPIO 39 | ST_RX | Legacy Serial receive |
| GPIO 41 | ST_TX | Legacy Serial transmit |
| GPIO 43 | ESP_TX | Debug UART0 TX |
| GPIO 44 | ESP_RX | Debug UART0 RX |
| GPIO 47 | DISP_RX | Display UART RX (from display to ESP32) |
| GPIO 48 | DISP_TX | Display UART TX (from ESP32 to display) |

ESP32-S3 module enable is routed as `ESP_EN`. GPIO 19/20 are reserved for USB D−/D+. GPIO 3, 35–37, 45, and 46 are unused.

## I²C Addresses

| Device | I²C Address | Ref | Notes |
|--------|------------|-----|-------|
| INA219 (current sense) | `0x40` | U2 | Bus voltage and current monitor; shunt = 0.33 Ω, full-scale 242 mA |
| OPT3004 (ambient light) | `0x44` | U12 | Used to auto-adjust display brightness |
| TMP112 (temperature) | `0x48` | U13 | Board temperature, ±0.5 °C accuracy |

All three sensors share the VCC 3.3 V supply and the common I²C bus (GPIO 8 SCL, GPIO 18 SDA) with 10 kΩ pull-ups to VCC.

## Power Rails

| Rail | Voltage | Source | Notes |
|------|---------|--------|-------|
| VSD | ~12 V (NMEA 2000 bus) | J2 NMEA2000 connector | Raw bus input; SMPS domain |
| VCC | 3.3 V | U1 LMR51610 buck + U4 HT7833 LDO | Digital domain supply (ESP32, sensors) |
| VDD | 5.0 V | U6 LMR51610 buck | Display domain supply |
| VDSP | 5.0 V | VDD via Q4 power switch | Switched display supply; enabled by DISP_EN |
| VSC | ~12 V | VSD via FB4 ferrite bead | Isolated CAN domain supply |
| VST | 12 V | U11 ZXTR2012 LDO | Isolated Legacy Serial TX supply |
| VS+ | Bus voltage | Direct from bus | Sensed by INA219 (U2) |
| GND_ST | — | Isolated ground | Legacy Serial isolated return |

VCC and VDD are generated by TI LMR51610 synchronous buck converters operating from VSD. The HT7833 LDO (U4) provides VCC during initial power-up before the DC-DC converter starts. The VST 12 V rail is generated by a ZXTR2012 LDO from the incoming bus line through an isolation barrier.

### Board Stackup

4-layer FR4 board with ENIG surface finish and dark navy epoxy solder mask.

| Layer | Type | Thickness |
|-------|------|-----------|
| F.SilkS | Top silkscreen | — |
| F.Mask | Top solder mask (epoxy) | 0.012 mm |
| F.Cu | Top copper (0.5 oz) | 0.0175 mm |
| dielectric 1 | FR4 prepreg | 0.1855 mm |
| In1.Cu | Inner copper (1 oz) | 0.035 mm |
| dielectric 2 | FR4 core | 1.1 mm |
| In2.Cu | Inner copper (1 oz) | 0.035 mm |
| dielectric 3 | FR4 prepreg | 0.1855 mm |
| B.Cu | Bottom copper (0.5 oz) | 0.0175 mm |
| B.Mask | Bottom solder mask (epoxy) | 0.012 mm |

Total board thickness: approximately 1.6 mm.

### Isolation

The design implements three isolated power domains — SMPS, CAN, and Digital — separated by ferrite beads (FB1–FB5) and optoisolators (U7–U9). The Legacy Serial domain uses galvanic isolation via optoisolators with a floating 12 V supply generated on the isolated side. Isolation barriers are marked on the power supplies schematic.

### Layout Notes

- The CAN bus common-mode filter (FL1) and ESD clamp (U10) must be placed close to the NMEA 2000 connector J2.
- Bypass capacitors for U3 (ESP32) must be placed as close as possible to the module pads.
- The INA219 shunt resistor R33 (0.33 Ω) carries the full bus current; Kelvin (4-wire) routing is required for accurate measurement.
- Legacy Serial TX optoisolators U8/U9 pin 6 connections require close placement — noted in the schematic.
- No vias within the ESP32 module keep-out area.

### Fiducials and Tooling

Four fiducial markers (FID1–FID4) are placed at the board corners to support AOI and pick-and-place alignment.

### Board Dimensions

95.2 mm × 95.2 mm.

### Compliance Markings

The board carries CE, FCC, UKCA, and RoHS silkscreen marks along with a QR code linking to the product documentation.
